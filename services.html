Services & Pricing

I’m not a company — just a guy who loves keeping these old consoles alive. Below is a breakdown of what I offer, how the process works, and what you can expect when you bring your console in for service.

1. Diagnostic Fee

Price: $25

Every repair starts with a diagnostic. I’ll inspect and test your console to determine what’s wrong. The fee covers the time spent testing and evaluating — but if you decide to go forward with a repair or restoration, this $25 is deducted from your total cost.

2. Maintenance / Restoration Package

Price: $65

This is my most popular option — perfect for an Xbox that still works but needs some TLC to stay that way. It includes a full teardown, cleaning, and replacement of key wear components.

  • 🧹 Blow out dust and debris
  • 🧼 Clean plastic components (shell, buttons)
  • 🔧 Clean fan and power supply
  • 💿 Clean DVD drive and replace drive belt
  • 🔋 Inspect capacitors and replace clock capacitor
  • 🪛 Clean motherboard and components
  • ❄️ Replace CPU and GPU thermal paste

This restores your Xbox to peak performance and gives it a fresh start for the next 20 years.

3. Additional Repairs (Outside the Package)

If your console has specific issues beyond general restoration, here’s a list of common repair services.

Repair Price
Capacitor replacement $5 per capacitor (minimum $15)
Disk drive replacement $60
Power supply replacement $60
Controller port replacement $25

Prices include both parts and labor. Costs may vary depending on part availability or complexity.

4. Optional Extras

Extra Price
Softmodding and Harddrive Upgrade $40+
Priority / Rush Service +$20–$50

Want to give your Xbox a unique look or need it done fast? These options let you personalize or expedite your service.

How It Works

  1. Bring in your Xbox and pay the $25 diagnostic fee.
  2. I inspect, test, and identify any issues.
  3. We discuss recommended repairs and costs.
  4. If you proceed, the $25 diagnostic is applied to your total.

Transparent pricing. No surprises — just honest work and a clean, working console at the end.
