🧰 Restoration Details

This console has been fully serviced under the Maintenance & Restoration Package by JB Old Skool Games. Below is a record of all the work performed to restore and preserve your original Xbox for years to come.

What Was Done

  • 💨 Blowout dust and debris
  • 🧽 Clean plastic components (shell, buttons, ports)
  • 🌬️ Clean fan for optimal airflow
  • 💿 Clean DVD drive and replace DVD drive belt
  • 🔍 Inspect capacitors for leakage or swelling
  • 🕒 Replace clock capacitor
  • 🧼 Clean motherboard and power board
  • ❄️ Replace CPU and GPU thermal paste
  • ⚡ Clean and inspect power supply

Final Testing

After reassembly, the console was tested for:

  • ✅ Stable power and video output
  • ✅ Reliable disc drive performance
  • ✅ Proper fan operation and temperature control
  • ✅ Controller port functionality
  • ✅ Memory and hard drive read/write stability

Care Tips

Your Xbox has been restored for long-term reliability — here are a few tips to keep it running strong:

  • Keep it in a cool, dry place to prevent capacitor wear and corrosion.
  • Clean exterior dust occasionally using a soft brush or compressed air.
  • Use a surge protector to safeguard the power supply.

Questions or Issues?

These consoles are 20+ years old, and while they’ve been fully restored, vintage hardware can sometimes surprise you. If you ever experience an issue, please reach out — I’m always happy to help.
